# Guess Who – Heroes of the Storm Edition

A digital version of the classic Guess Who board game featuring Heroes of the Storm characters!

## About

Two commanders. One mystery hero. Ask yes/no questions to identify your opponent's chosen champion before they reveal yours. The Nexus awaits.

## Game Features

- **3 Characters to Choose From:** Arthas, Jaina, Tyrael, Sylvanas, Malfurion, Thrall, Brightwing, Illidan, Stitches, Jim Raynor, Kerrigan, Artanis, Abathur, Zagara, Nova, Diablo, Li-Ming, Johanna, Nazeebo, Sonya, Tracer, Lúcio, Ana, Genji
- **AI Opponent:** Choose difficulty levels (Recruit, Veteran, Heroic)
- **Question System:** Ask questions about universe, role, gender, abilities, and more
- **Battle Log:** Track all questions and answers
- **Responsive Design:** Works on desktop and mobile

## How to Play

1. Select AI difficulty (Recruit/Veteran/Heroic)
2. Enter the Nexus to start
3. Click heroes to eliminate them from your board
4. Ask questions like "Warcraft?", "Female?", "Ranged?"
5. Make guesses when you're confident
6. Win by identifying the AI's hero first!
